Email Support Membership Guide

The Nerdy Neighbor Club offers affordable, ongoing tech help by email. It’s ideal for quick advice, basic setup guidance, password help, or general tech questions.

How It Works

  • You email your question or issue to clubhelp@yournerdyneighbor.com
  • I reply personally, within 1 business day (often faster, but never more than 24 hours)
  • If something can’t be solved by email, we’ll schedule a visit

What It’s Great For

  • “How do I...” style questions
  • Email or account setup help
  • Password resets or recovery
  • Advice on which device to buy or use
  • Quick clarifications from a past visit

What It’s Not For

  • In-depth troubleshooting
  • Urgent or emergency problems
  • Issues that clearly require hands-on work

If it looks like a home visit or off-site repair is needed, we’ll talk about options before going further. Club members always get priority scheduling and a discount on visits.

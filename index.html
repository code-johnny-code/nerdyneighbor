Reasonable Expectations

I do my best to treat every device like it’s my own, and most of the time, everything goes just fine. That said, technology can be unpredictable—especially when it’s already acting up.

When you invite me into your home or hand off a device, you’re trusting me to give it my full attention, care, and honesty. I’ll never push something you don’t need, and I’ll always talk through the plan before doing anything extra.

What I Promise

  • I’ll show up when I say I will
  • I’ll explain things clearly and honestly
  • I’ll respect your home and your time
  • If I can’t fix it, I’ll tell you straight
  • If I make a mistake, I’ll make it right

What You Should Know

  • I can’t guarantee I’ll be able to fix everything
  • Sometimes devices fail on their own, especially if they’re already on their way out
  • Data loss, manufacturer defects, or internet outages might be outside my control

If I need to take something with me to work on, we’ll agree on what needs doing first—and I’ll keep you in the loop every step of the way.

My goal is always the same: solve the problem, keep it simple, and make sure you feel good about the help you got.
